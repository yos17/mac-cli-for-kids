SUMMARY OF INCIDENT:
On the night of March 14–15, suspect M.K., with the assistance of
former employee K. Mercer, conducted an unauthorized entry into the
Terminal Detective Agency's evidence room. The suspects targeted files
from Case #002 and disrupted other active evidence.

CONFIRMED EVIDENCE:
- Keycard of K. Mercer used to bypass supply closet security at 11:51 PM
- Fingerprint of K. Mercer recovered from Filing Cabinet C-7 (94% match)
- Boot print (size 11, TrailMaster brand) confirmed match to earlier scene
- Security footage places subject inside building for 23 minutes
- Fiber evidence links K. Mercer to blue jacket seen by three witnesses

CONCLUSION:
This was a coordinated, premeditated operation. Suspect had knowledge
of Agency layout, alarm codes, and evidence storage. Investigation into
potential additional moles within the Agency is ongoing.

Warrant issued. Pursuit authorized. Case status: ACTIVE.
