MISSION: BUILD YOUR DETECTIVE BOT (MyBot)
------------------------------------------
The mystery_solution_template.sh file is a starter script for
your personal detective assistant. It already has a menu, a
morning briefing, and a framework — but it needs YOU to finish it.

Open the file, read the TODO comments, and make it yours:
  nano mystery_solution_template.sh

When you're done customizing it, make it runnable:
  chmod +x mystery_solution_template.sh

Then launch your bot:
  ./mystery_solution_template.sh

COLLECT THE FINAL PIECE
------------------------
Check all_clues.txt to review all 11 code pieces you've collected.
Then find the final secret_code_piece.txt to get the 12th word.

UNLOCK THE ULTIMATE TREASURE
-----------------------------
Once you have all 12 pieces, open FINAL_TREASURE.txt:
