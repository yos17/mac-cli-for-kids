================================================================
DETECTIVE'S PERSONAL DIARY — STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
Day 5 of the Missing USB Drive Case
================================================================

Day 5: Cross-referencing complete. My suspect list just got shorter.

Network logs show only two people were logged into the Academy
network between 13:15 and 13:30 from terminals near Room 4B:
  1. Dr. Felix Okafor (Suspect #1) — but he was logged in from
     his OFFICE, which is on the complete opposite side of the
     building from Room 4B. Cleared.
  2. Agent Reyna Vasquez (Suspect #2) — her terminal session
     shows she was logged in from Room 4B, Station 6.

Station 6 is right next to Station 7, where I found the USB cap.

I brought Vasquez back in for a second interview. She seemed
nervous this time — not calm like before. She kept touching her
lanyard. I asked her directly: "Were you in Room 4B on the day
the USB went missing?"

She paused. Three full seconds. Then: "I might have been. I use
those computers sometimes."

That pause. That hesitation. That's the tell.

But here's the thing — I still haven't found the actual USB drive.
Accessing it isn't the same as stealing it. Maybe she used it and
put it back... and it went missing after that?

Technician Bob is still on my list, just for that comment about
the blue cap. Something is off about him.

Also — I keep forgetting to bring my coat to work. It's been cold.

— Detective J.
