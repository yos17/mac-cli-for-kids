Date: February 19, 2024
Day 37 of Operation: Sparkle Heist

Dear Diary,

I think someone moved my cheese.

I know that sounds like a metaphor, but I mean literally —
my cheese was on the left side of the fridge and now it's
on the right side. I live alone, Diary. I am the only one
with a key to this lair.

Unless.

UNLESS SOMEONE HAS A COPY OF MY KEY.

I changed all my locks today. It took four hours and I
accidentally locked myself in the bathroom for forty
minutes, but the important thing is the locks are changed.

I am also QUITE SURE someone has been reading my papers.
I always leave them at a 7-degree angle (I measured it with
a protractor) and this morning they were definitely at
closer to 9 degrees. Maybe 8.5 at best.

The Terminal Detective Agency is good. Very good.
But they haven't found the library hiding spot. I checked.
Volume 11 is undisturbed.

Still, I must be more careful. I've started writing my
plans in a secret code (every third letter reversed, then
add 2 to each vowel — very sophisticated).

This diary entry is NOT in that code, unfortunately.
I forgot.

— Dr. C. Bumblebee III

P.S. It was probably just the cheese rolling. Cheese rolls.
     I knew this. I panicked unnecessarily. Again.
