TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R004
Filed by: Agent Kowalski
Date: February 7, 2024
Subject: The Phantom of the Parking Lot

SUMMARY:
Staff at the Riverside Shopping Center report that someone
has been rearranging shopping trolleys into elaborate
patterns every Tuesday night for the past three weeks.
The patterns resemble constellations.

No property has been damaged. The trolleys are always
found clean and neatly arranged. Staff are confused.

FINDINGS:
- Security footage shows a person in a large coat and hat
- Person arrives at 11:15pm, finishes by midnight
- The arrangement on week 3 was identified by Dr. Huang
  of the City Planetarium as "a perfect Orion's Belt"
- Coffee cup found nearby: large, with "C.B." written on it
  in what appears to be villain-themed handwriting (very
  dramatic loops on the letters)

CURRENT STATUS:
No complaint filed — shopping center staff actually think
it's "quite artistic." Case being kept open for monitoring.

NEXT STEPS:
Continue surveillance on Tuesday nights.
Check initials "C.B." against known subjects.

Filed: February 7, 2024
Status: MONITORING
