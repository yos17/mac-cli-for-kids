DETECTIVE DIARY — DAY 1
=======================

Dear Diary,

Today I received my official TDA badge from Director Chen herself.
It came in a small velvet box with a note that said:
"The best detectives ask the best questions."

I have been assigned a desk in the corner of the office.
The Terminal on my desk glows green. I love it.

Agent CIPHER showed me how to read files with cat.
I practiced for three hours. My eyes hurt a little.

But I found something important in an old case file —
a pattern nobody had noticed before. Director Chen
looked impressed. She said: "Good eye, Rookie."

I am going to be a great detective.

First official entry complete.
Tomorrow: stakeout training with Agent SWIFT.
