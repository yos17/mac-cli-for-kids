TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R002
Filed by: Agent Torres
Date: February 3, 2024
Subject: The Case of the Stolen Cookie Jar

SUMMARY:
The Maple Hill Elementary School reported that the famous
"Cookie Jar Trophy" (awarded annually to the best baker
in the school's bake-off) went missing from the display
case in the main hallway.

The trophy is shaped like a giant chocolate chip cookie
and is made of solid bronze. Estimated weight: 4kg.

FINDINGS:
- Display case was not broken — opened with a key
- Only 3 people have keys: Principal Davies, Janitor Bob,
  and the current trophy holder, student Maya Chen (age 11)
- Chocolate chip crumbs found at the scene
- Janitor Bob was on holiday in Florida (CONFIRMED alibi)

CURRENT STATUS:
Maya Chen interviewed. She seemed very upset and denied
all involvement. Principal Davies questioned — cooperating.

NEXT STEPS:
Check school security camera footage (if any).
Interview all students who entered hallway Feb 1-2.
