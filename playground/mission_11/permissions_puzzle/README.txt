PERMISSIONS PUZZLE
==================
The files below have been locked by the suspect!
You need to use chmod to unlock each one.

Step 1: Try reading level1.txt — you'll get "Permission denied"!
        First, run: chmod 000 level1.txt level2.txt level3.txt
        This locks all three files.

Step 2: Unlock level1.txt:
        chmod 644 level1.txt
        cat level1.txt

Step 3: level1.txt will tell you how to unlock level2.txt

Step 4: level2.txt will tell you how to unlock level3.txt

Step 5: level3.txt contains a special message!
