DETECTIVE'S FIELD NOTES — MONDAY

WITNESS 1 (name withheld): Works the late shift at the diner across the
street. Saw a figure in a dark blue jacket pacing near the east exit

That's too consistent to be coincidence. The east exit theory is holding.
