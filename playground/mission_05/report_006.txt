TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R006
Filed by: Agent Patel
Date: February 10, 2024
Subject: Unauthorized Use of the City Fountain

SUMMARY:
City maintenance reports that someone added approximately
2 liters of bubble bath solution to the main city fountain
at Central Park. The fountain ran bubbles for 6 hours.
No structural damage. Several ducks seemed to enjoy it.

FINDINGS:
- Bubble bath brand: "LuxeBubble Lavender Dream"
- Purchased at corner pharmacy on Fifth Street
- Pharmacy receipt found in nearby trash can, signed "Z"
- Security footage unclear due to heavy coat and hat

CURRENT STATUS:
City has decided not to press charges. The mayor's office
says the fountain "looked rather festive, actually."

This is possibly the most cheerful crime we've ever
investigated. Adding to linked cases file anyway.

NEXT STEPS:
Identify "Z" — this initial appears in multiple cases.
