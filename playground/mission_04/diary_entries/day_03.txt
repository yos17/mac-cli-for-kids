DETECTIVE DIARY — DAY 3
=======================

I solved the sandwich mystery.

Using grep, I searched through 200 lines of break room access logs
in approximately 4 seconds. Four. Seconds.

The culprit: Agent NOVA.
She genuinely believed the communal fridge was for sharing.
She was not malicious. She was just confused about ownership.

Director Chen made her buy everyone lunch as apology.
I had a club sandwich. It was excellent.

LESSON LEARNED:
grep is the most powerful tool I have encountered so far.
It can search a file for any word or pattern.
It can search ALL files in a folder at once.
It is like a superpower for finding things.

I asked Agent CIPHER what her favorite command was.
She said grep without hesitating.

I understand now.

Also: I almost deleted my entire notes folder with rm.
I typed rm instead of rm -i and caught myself just in time.
From now on: ALWAYS look before you rm.
