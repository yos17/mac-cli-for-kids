CASE BRIEFING
=============

Agent, every great detective needs a custom headquarters.

Think about it — James Bond had his gadget lab. Sherlock Holmes had
221B Baker Street. Batman had the Batcave.

You have your TERMINAL.

Your mission: set up your terminal as your personal detective
command center! Customize your .zshrc with detective aliases,
add a cool prompt, and make your terminal look like a real spy
station.

The best part? Once you customize it, your terminal will greet
you like a secret base EVERY TIME you open it.

YOUR LAIR, YOUR RULES.

MISSION OBJECTIVES:
-------------------
1. Explore the sample_zshrc file to see detective customizations
2. Pick aliases from aliases.txt that you like
3. Choose a theme from the themes/ folder
4. Add your favorites to your real ~/.zshrc file
5. Reload with: source ~/.zshrc
6. Watch your terminal transform!

FILES IN THIS FOLDER:
---------------------
  sample_zshrc          — A ready-made detective .zshrc to study
  aliases.txt           — Cool alias ideas to add to your config
  themes/               — Three terminal themes to choose from
  secret_code_piece.txt — Your mission reward!

HINT: To edit your real .zshrc, type:
  open -a TextEdit ~/.zshrc

Or if you're feeling brave, use nano:
  nano ~/.zshrc

Good luck, Agent. Make your lair legendary.
