DETECTIVE'S PERSONAL DIARY — STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
Day 6 of the Missing USB Drive Case
================================================================

Day 6: Commander Chen is asking for a progress report tomorrow.

I have circumstantial evidence pointing at Vasquez. I have a
suspicious Technician Bob. I have a desk thread, a sticky note,
a dropped USB cap, and six days of notes.

What I DO NOT have: the USB drive itself.

I went back to the original desk one more time. This is the sixth
time I've examined it. I opened every drawer. I looked under the
keyboard. I lifted the monitor.

Nothing.

I called the owner of the desk — a researcher named Dr. Amari — who
has been out of town all week at a conference. She was surprised to
hear the USB was missing. "I always keep it in my coat pocket," she
said. "Or on the dock. Check the dock first."

"We checked the dock," I said. "It wasn't there."

"Hmm," she said. "Did you check my coat? The navy one, hanging in
the break room? I've been meaning to pick it up for a week."

The COAT.

"Don't forget the coat." That note — written BY Dr. Amari,
TO HERSELF, as a reminder to pick up her coat from the break room.

The USB drive was in her coat pocket.

I ran to the break room.

The coat was still hanging there. The pocket was...
