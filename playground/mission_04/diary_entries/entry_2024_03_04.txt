Date: March 4, 2024
Day 50 of Operation: Sparkle Heist

Dear Diary,

The escape plan is ready.

In exactly two weeks, after the final exchange at the clock
tower, I will disappear. I have prepared three bags:

BAG 1: Essentials (toothbrush, good socks, the penguin book,
       my entire cheese collection, important documents)

BAG 2: Villainous equipment (I will not list this here,
       even in my private diary, because I have learned
       my lesson about writing things down)

BAG 3: Backup essentials (more cheese, mostly)

I have a train ticket booked under the name "Mr. Fluffington"
to the coast. From there, a boat. From there, who knows!
Adventure! Freedom! A new life!

Z has arranged safe passage. Z knows a person who knows a
person who knows a boat captain named Dave.

Dave apparently makes excellent sandwiches AND can navigate
by the stars. Z says Dave is very reliable.

I find myself oddly excited, Diary. After all these years
of scheming and plotting and accidentally locking myself
out of things, freedom is finally within reach.

Two more weeks.

— Dr. C. Bumblebee III, soon to be Mr. Fluffington

P.S. I finished the penguin book. Excellent ending.
