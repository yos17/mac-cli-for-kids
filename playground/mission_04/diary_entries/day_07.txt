DETECTIVE DIARY — DAY 7
=======================

Last entry before the big operation.

Tomorrow the full team deploys. The target: a suspect known only
as "The Architect." They've been hiding data across dozens of servers
using encrypted files and misleading filenames.

Director Chen briefed us at 0700.
She said: "We find them because we are better at searching
than they are at hiding."

I have been practicing all week.
  cat — read any file
  less — scroll through long ones
  head/tail — peek at beginning or end
  grep — find anything, anywhere, in seconds
  >> and > — write to files without losing data

I am ready.

One more thing: I found an encrypted clue in the case file.
It was base64 encoded. I used:   echo "[code]" | base64 -d
And it decoded instantly.

The clue said something about a RED hat.
I will follow up on this tomorrow.

Wish me luck, Diary.
This is what all the training was for.
