TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R010
Filed by: Agent Kowalski
Date: February 18, 2024
Subject: Stolen Recipe from Baking Competition

SUMMARY:
Mr. Ferdinand Plum, three-time winner of the Regional
Scone Championship, reports that his secret recipe has
been stolen from his kitchen.

The recipe — for "Plum's Legendary Lavender Scones" —
was kept in a locked recipe box. The box was found open
but not damaged, suggesting a key or skilled lockpicking.

FINDINGS:
- No fingerprints on the box (gloves used)
- A single lavender flower left on the counter
  (theatrical and unnecessary, but noted)
- Mr. Plum's neighbor, Mrs. Wimble, has entered the
  same baking competition for the past 6 years without
  placing in the top 10
- Mrs. Wimble's garden: full of lavender

CURRENT STATUS:
Mrs. Wimble questioned. Denied everything emphatically.
Said the lavender was "coincidental." Seemed nervous.

NEXT STEPS:
Compare Mrs. Wimble's scone recipe (if she enters) with
the stolen Plum recipe after competition on March 3.

Filed: February 18, 2024
Status: OPEN — competition date pending
