TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R017
Filed by: Agent Torres
Date: March 3, 2024
Subject: Scone Competition Follow-Up (Case R010)

SUMMARY:
The Regional Scone Championship was held today at the
Riverside Community Hall. Update on Case TDA-2024-R010.

FINDINGS:
Mrs. Wimble entered the competition with a scone described
by all three judges as "suspiciously perfect." The recipe
appeared nearly identical to Mr. Plum's Legendary Lavender
Scones in technique, ratios, and lavender application.

However: Mr. Plum did NOT enter the competition.
He said he had "lost his enthusiasm for competitive baking"
and stayed home to watch television.

CURRENT STATUS:
Mrs. Wimble won first place.
Mr. Plum does not wish to press charges — he says the
scones were "clearly better than mine anyway, somehow,"
and that he respects the craftsmanship.

Case closed at Mr. Plum's request.

NOTES:
This outcome was unexpected but peaceful. Mrs. Wimble
did bring Mr. Plum a box of the winning scones as a
goodwill gesture. He ate six of them.
