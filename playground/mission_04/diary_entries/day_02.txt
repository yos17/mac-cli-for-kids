================================================================
DETECTIVE'S PERSONAL DIARY — STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
Day 2 of the Missing USB Drive Case
================================================================

Day 2: The fingerprint was a dead end. It belongs to the janitor.

Checked the corridor surveillance footage. The camera near the target
desk was — of course — pointing at the ceiling due to a "maintenance
adjustment" last Thursday. Classic. Either the worst timing in the
world, or the best timing someone could have planned.

I interviewed Suspect #1 today: Dr. Felix Okafor, the Academy's
lead technology instructor. His alibi is that he was "in a meeting."
I checked — there was indeed a meeting. He was at it. But the meeting
ended 45 minutes BEFORE the USB was discovered missing.

That 45-minute gap is interesting.

Also interesting: Dr. Okafor's coffee mug was sitting on the target
desk. "Oh, I borrowed the charger," he said when I asked. Plausible.
But I filed it under "interesting."

I found something else: a small sticky note on the underside of the
desk drawer. It said: "Don't forget the coat."

Whose handwriting is it? Unknown. What does it mean? Also unknown.
But detectives collect everything.

Note to self: Find out who wrote that sticky note.

— Detective J.
