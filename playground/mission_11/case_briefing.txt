Agent, we've intercepted encrypted files from the suspect!

---------------------
Five messages encoded in base64. The suspect thought this would
keep us out. Think again. Use this command to decode them:

  base64 -d classified/message_01.txt

Work through all five. One of them contains something very
important to YOU personally...

THE SECRET MESSAGES FOLDER

Intercepted plain-text communications between agents in the
field. Read them carefully — they paint a picture of what's
really going on.

  cat secret_messages/from_agent_z.txt

----------------------
The suspect locked three files with broken permissions.
You'll need chmod to break through!

  Read permissions_puzzle/README.txt to get started.
