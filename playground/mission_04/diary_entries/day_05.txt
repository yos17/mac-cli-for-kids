DETECTIVE DIARY — DAY 5
=======================

I used the less command today to scroll through a massive file.
200 pages of surveillance transcripts.

It took a while. But I found something.
On page 147, one witness mentioned the name "Veracruz."
Nobody had flagged it. The previous analyst missed it entirely.

Why? Because they were reading it by hand, line by line.
I searched with / in less and found it in seconds.

Director Chen promoted me from Rookie to Junior Agent.
I now have a real codename pending: TBD.

Agent CIPHER said the / search in less is "the detective's shortcut."
She is right about everything.

Also learned: head shows the first lines of a file.
tail shows the last lines.
tail -f watches a file in real time as new lines are added.
This is incredibly useful for watching log files during an operation.

Tomorrow: the Veracruz lead gets investigated.
I will need grep. Definitely grep.
