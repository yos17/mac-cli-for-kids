TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R001
Filed by: Agent Kowalski
Date: February 1, 2024
Subject: The Case of the Missing Garden Gnome

SUMMARY:
Mrs. Harrington of 14 Buttercup Lane reported her beloved
garden gnome "Gerald Jr." missing on January 30, 2024.
Gerald Jr. has been in the family for 11 years and is
described as "irreplaceable" by the homeowner.

FINDINGS:
- Muddy boot prints (size 9) found near the garden
- Tire tracks suggest a bicycle, not a car
- Neighbor spotted a "suspicious figure in a raincoat"
  at approximately 7:30pm the previous evening

CURRENT STATUS:
Investigation ongoing. No suspects identified yet.
Cross-referencing boot print database.

NEXT STEPS:
Interview neighbors on Buttercup Lane (houses 12-18).
Check local pawn shops for ceramic garden ornaments.
