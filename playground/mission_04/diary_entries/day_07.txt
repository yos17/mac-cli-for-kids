DETECTIVE'S PERSONAL DIARY — STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
Day 7 of the Missing USB Drive Case — CASE CLOSED
================================================================

Day 7: Case closed. I am not sure whether to laugh or cry.

I ran to the break room. I checked the coat — the navy wool Academy
coat hanging on the third hook from the window. I reached into the
left pocket.

Nothing.

I checked the right pocket.

A USB drive with a blue cap.

FOUND IT.

The USB drive was in Dr. Amari's coat pocket. The coat she'd left in
the break room a week ago and kept forgetting to pick up. The USB
was there the entire time. It had never been stolen. Nobody had taken
it. The "missing" USB was just... in a coat pocket.

So what about all my "evidence"?
  - Dr. Okafor's coffee mug: he really did just borrow the charger.
  - Agent Vasquez in Room 4B: she was doing her own research project.
  - Technician Bob knowing about the blue cap: Dr. Amari mentioned
    the color when she reported it missing, and Bob was in the room.
  - The dropped USB cap in Room 4B: unrelated USB drive. They're
    everywhere. I was seeing connections that weren't there.

The moral of this story: sometimes the answer to a mystery is not a
criminal mastermind. Sometimes it's a coat pocket.

I told Commander Chen. She laughed for about 45 seconds. Then she
said: "This is actually a GREAT lesson, Detective. The best detectives
investigate what IS, not just what SEEMS. You followed the evidence.
The evidence led you to the truth. That's the job."

I am choosing to take that as a compliment.

Case #USB-2026-MISSING: CLOSED — ITEM RECOVERED, NO FOUL PLAY.

Note to self: I found my OWN coat today while I was there. It was
right next to Dr. Amari's. I've been looking for it all week too.

Some detective.

— Detective J.
