DETECTIVE'S PERSONAL DIARY — STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
Day 4 of the Missing USB Drive Case
================================================================

Day 4: A breakthrough! And then, immediately, a setback.

The dark thread from the desk: I sent it to Agent Sparks in the
forensics lab. Results came back fast: wool blend, dark navy,
consistent with Academy-issue winter coats. Which means it could
literally be anyone who works here. Half the Academy wears the
same coat in winter.

The setback.

BUT — while waiting for those results, I found something new.
Someone accessed the USB drive database at 13:22 on the day it
went missing. The access log shows the drive was plugged into a
computer in Room 4B.

Room 4B is a common-use computer lab. No login required. No cameras.
But I found something on the floor underneath station 7 in Room 4B:
a small blue rubber cap. The same kind that fits on the end of a
USB drive.

Someone took the drive there, used it, and dropped the cap.

Who uses Room 4B? Everyone. But the access log narrows the timing.
I need to cross-reference who was logged into the Academy network
between 13:15 and 13:30 on that day.

"Don't forget the coat." I keep thinking about that note.
Maybe it's not a clue about where the USB IS.
Maybe it's a reminder to someone — the OWNER — about where
they last put it.

— Detective J.
