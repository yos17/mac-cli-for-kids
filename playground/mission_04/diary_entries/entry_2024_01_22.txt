Date: January 22, 2024
Day 8 of Operation: Sparkle Heist

Dear Diary,

I have a bad feeling about the Terminal Detective Agency.

I overheard two people at the coffee shop this morning talking
about a "new agent" who is "really good at finding things."
One of them said she could find a needle in a digital haystack.

A DIGITAL HAYSTACK, Diary.

That is exactly where I have hidden my secrets! Well, not a
literal digital haystack, but you know what I mean.

I tried to look casual while eavesdropping. I crossed my legs,
pretended to read my newspaper, and accidentally knocked over
my hot chocolate. It went everywhere. Very embarrassing.

I must be more careful. Perhaps I should vary my routes to the
secret lair. And I should definitely stop wearing this enormous
cape in public. People keep staring.

On the bright side: my plan is still proceeding nicely.
The files remain hidden. No one suspects a thing.

(Except possibly the barista. She gave me a very suspicious
look when I ordered my "secret villain latte." I thought it
sounded cool. I was wrong.)

— Dr. C. Bumblebee III

P.S. Must remember: capes are for indoors only.
