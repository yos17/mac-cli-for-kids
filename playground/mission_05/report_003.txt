TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R003
Filed by: Agent Patel
Date: February 5, 2024
Subject: Mysterious Footprints on Crumpet Lane

SUMMARY:
Multiple residents of Crumpet Lane reported finding strange
footprints outside their homes on the morning of Feb 4.
The footprints appear to belong to someone wearing very
large shoes — estimated size 14 — and they travel up
and down the full length of the street three times.

FINDINGS:
- Footprints made by rubber-soled shoes
- Pattern suggests the person was walking SLOWLY, not running
- The footprints stop abruptly outside #7 Crumpet Lane
  (the abandoned balloon shop)
- No footprints going away from #7 — subject may have
  entered through the front door

CURRENT STATUS:
The balloon shop was checked. No sign of current occupancy,
however: a fresh cheese wrapper was found near the entrance.
Brand: "Fancy Fromage — Premium Gouda."

NEXT STEPS:
Obtain warrant to search balloon shop interior.
Check Fancy Fromage purchase records (long shot).
