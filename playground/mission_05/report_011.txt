TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R011
Filed by: Agent Torres
Date: February 20, 2024
Subject: Neighborhood Cat Mystery

SUMMARY:
Seven cats in the Elm Grove neighborhood have simultaneously
decided to sit in front of #4 Mulberry Court every evening
at sunset. The homeowner (Mr. Grundy, age 78) reports he
does not own any cats and has not done anything to attract
them. He finds it "somewhat unnerving."

The cats are all different breeds and belong to different
owners across a 4-block radius.

FINDINGS:
- Investigation reveals that Mr. Grundy accidentally
  spilled a can of premium tuna oil near his front step
  three weeks ago
- The stain is not visible but is apparently very obvious
  to cats
- No criminal activity detected

CURRENT STATUS:
Mr. Grundy's step cleaned with specialist solution.
Cats dispersed within 24 hours.
Case closed. Everyone is fine.

NOTES:
This is the seventh cat-related case this year.
Agent Torres requests a transfer to cybercrime.

Filed: February 20, 2024
Status: CLOSED
