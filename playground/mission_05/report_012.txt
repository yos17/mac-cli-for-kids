TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R012
Filed by: Agent Patel
Date: February 22, 2024
Subject: Fake Museum Exhibit

SUMMARY:
The City History Museum reports that someone has added
an unauthorized exhibit to Gallery B without permission.

The exhibit consists of:
- A framed certificate declaring the subject "Most
  Ingenious Villain of the Year (Self-Awarded)"
- A photo of a person in a cape (face obscured)
- A small card reading: "Donated by Dr. C.B. — For posterity"

The exhibit was up for three days before staff noticed.
An estimated 240 visitors saw it. Several left positive
comments in the guest book.

FINDINGS:
- The frame was hung with picture hooks already in the wall
  from a previous exhibit (no new holes drilled)
- The certificate paper matches the VelvetScript brand
  from Case TDA-2024-R009
- "Dr. C.B." initials consistent with known subject

CURRENT STATUS:
Exhibit removed. Museum slightly annoyed but oddly amused.
Keeping the guest book comments as evidence/entertainment.

Filed: February 22, 2024
Status: CLOSED — no formal complaint filed
