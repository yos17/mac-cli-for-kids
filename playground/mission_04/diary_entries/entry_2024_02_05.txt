Date: February 5, 2024
Day 23 of Operation: Sparkle Heist

Dear Diary,

I am being FOLLOWED.

At least, I think I am. There was a person in a blue hat
behind me at the grocery store. Then the same blue hat
appeared outside my lair (the abandoned balloon shop on
Crumpet Lane — very good location, very affordable rent).

Could be a coincidence. Blue hats are very common.
Lots of people wear blue hats.

But THEN — and this is the suspicious part — I saw blue hat
AGAIN at the library. That cannot be a coincidence, Diary.
That is three times in one day!

I took evasive action: I ducked into the mystery novel
section and hid behind a very large book about penguins.
I waited for eleven minutes. When I emerged, blue hat
was gone.

HA! Outsmarted!

I must be more careful about my route to the library. I have
been going every Tuesday at 3pm, which in hindsight was not
the most secretive schedule for a secret villain.

Moving my library visits to random days from now on.

— Dr. C. Bumblebee III

P.S. The penguin book was actually very interesting.
     I may go back and finish it. Casually. Not suspiciously.
