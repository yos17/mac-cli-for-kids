TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R013
Filed by: Agent Kowalski
Date: February 24, 2024
Subject: The Case of the Backwards Street Signs

SUMMARY:
Public Works reports that three street signs in the
downtown area were reversed overnight — so they now
read backwards. No signs were removed or damaged.
They were simply turned 180 degrees.

Affected signs:
- "FIFTH STREET" now reads "TEERTS HTFIF"
- "CLOCK TOWER LANE" now reads "ENAL REWOT KCOLC"
- "CENTRAL LIBRARY RD" now reads "DR YRARBIIL LARTNEC"

FINDINGS:
- No tools required to rotate the signs (standard bolt)
- Done between midnight and 5am
- Witness (a night-shift nurse) reports seeing "someone
  in an enormous coat and no hat" near Clock Tower Lane
- This is the first time the subject has been seen
  WITHOUT the hat — description: "tallish, cheerful gait"

CURRENT STATUS:
Signs restored by Public Works within 2 hours.
Nurse providing updated sketch to our artist.

NOTES:
"Clock Tower Lane" reversed — this fits Clock Tower
connection in Case TDA-2024-004. Flag for that file.
