DETECTIVE DIARY — DAY 4
=======================

BIG day.

Agent BLADE taught me about hidden files today.
Files that START WITH A DOT are invisible to regular ls.
You have to use ls -la to see them.

I checked my own computer and found a folder called .old_passwords
from approximately three years ago.

Inside was a file with passwords including:
  password123
  myfavoritecat
  qwerty

I changed all of them immediately.

Director Chen says hidden files are used for configuration,
for secrets, for things that should not be casually visible.
"Hiding in plain sight," she called it.

The dot at the front is like a cloak.
Useful for good detectives. Also used by criminals.

That is why knowing about hidden files matters.

New mission tomorrow: the big one.
A real suspect. Real encrypted files.
Agent STORM says I am ready.

I am terrified and excited.
