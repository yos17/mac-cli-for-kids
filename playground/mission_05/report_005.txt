TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R005
Filed by: Agent Torres
Date: February 8, 2024
Subject: The Curious Case of the Singing Mailbox

SUMMARY:
Residents at 22 Fernwood Drive report that their mailbox
has been playing music every morning at exactly 6:45am.
The song is always the same: "Flight of the Bumblebee."

The mailbox is a standard metal box with no built-in
speakers, power source, or Bluetooth capability.

FINDINGS:
- A small wireless speaker (brand: TinyTune Pro) was
  discovered taped inside the mailbox lid
- Speaker was set to a timer
- The device was purchased online — order traced to
  a "Dr. C. Bumblebee" (address: the balloon shop)
- No note or explanation left at the scene

CURRENT STATUS:
Device removed and bagged as evidence.
Subject name matches initials from Case TDA-2024-R004.
Cases may be linked.

NOTES:
The residents at 22 Fernwood Drive actually enjoyed the
music and have asked if we could put it back.
We said no. They seem disappointed.
