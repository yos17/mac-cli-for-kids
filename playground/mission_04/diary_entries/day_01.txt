DETECTIVE'S PERSONAL DIARY — STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL
Day 1 of the Missing USB Drive Case
================================================================

Day 1: Started my first real case today. The Missing USB Drive case.

Commander Chen called me into her office at 0800 sharp. "We have a
situation," she said, which is code for "someone did something dumb."

A USB drive has gone missing. Not just any USB drive — this one
contains the access codes to the Academy's training simulation
database. If someone outside the Academy gets their hands on it,
they could reset every recruit's progress. All that training, gone.

Chen assigned it to me because (and I quote) "You're the only one
who hasn't lost something this week." High bar.

I started by examining the desk where the USB was last seen. Found
a partial fingerprint on the charging dock next to the monitor.
Could belong to the owner. Could belong to anyone who's visited the
desk in the last 48 hours.

Suspects identified so far: three colleagues who work in the same
wing. All had access. All have vague alibis.

This is going to be a long case.

Note to self: Check the surveillance footage from the main corridor.
And stop drinking coffee after 6pm — couldn't sleep last night.

— Detective J.
