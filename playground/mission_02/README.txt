Agent, we have a situation.

Someone broke into the Agency office overnight. They moved fast —
scattered our case files everywhere, hid evidence in subfolders,
and buried the most important clue FOUR levels deep inside the
office directory. By the time our security system triggered the
alarm, they were already gone.

We need YOU to navigate the office and recover all the evidence
before it falls into the wrong hands.

YOUR MISSION:

   1. Start in this folder and run: ls
      (See what's here at the top level)

   2. Move into the office/ folder: cd office
      Then look around again: ls

   3. Keep going deeper — into desk/, then drawer/, then secret/
      At each level, use ls to see what's there.

   4. Collect all 3 clues hidden throughout the office.

   5. Find the treasure map buried at the deepest level.

   6. Use pwd at any time to check WHERE you are.
      (pwd = "print working directory" — it tells you your location)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CLUE LOCATIONS (for your reference):

   Clue 1 — office/
   Clue 2 — office/desk/
   Clue 3 — archive/     (watch out — it's NOT under office!)
   Treasure Map — office/desk/drawer/secret/

     Try 'pwd' whenever you feel lost — it always tells you
     exactly where you are.

Good luck, Agent. The clock is ticking.
