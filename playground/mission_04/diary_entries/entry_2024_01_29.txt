Date: January 29, 2024
Day 15 of Operation: Sparkle Heist

Dear Diary,

I have acquired THE ITEM.

I cannot write down exactly what it is (in case this diary
falls into the wrong hands, i.e., any hands except mine),
but let's just say it is VERY important for Step 3 of my plan.

I found it at a little antique shop on the corner of Birch and
Fifth. The shopkeeper, a tiny old woman named Mrs. Figgins,
had absolutely no idea what she was selling. She just said
it was "a funny old thing" and charged me four dollars.

FOUR DOLLARS, Diary.

For an item of such IMMENSE VILLAINOUS VALUE.

I felt almost guilty. Almost.

I have hidden THE ITEM in a secure location (my sock drawer,
third from the left, under the argyle ones). No one would
ever look there.

Step 3 of the plan can now begin. Everything is coming
together beautifully.

Mwa ha ha.

(I've been practicing my villainous laugh. I think it's
getting better. Mrs. Figgins did give me a bit of a look
when I did it in her shop, though.)

— Dr. C. Bumblebee III
