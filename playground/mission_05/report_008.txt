TERMINAL DETECTIVE AGENCY — CASE REPORT
========================================
Report #: TDA-2024-R008
Filed by: Agent Torres
Date: February 14, 2024
Subject: Suspicious Suitcase at Train Station

SUMMARY:
Central Station security flagged a suitcase left
unattended on Platform 7 on February 13, 2024.

The bomb squad was called. The suitcase contained:
- 6 pairs of good socks (all argyle)
- One toothbrush (travel size, unused)
- A very large amount of cheese (assorted varieties)
- A library book about penguins (OVERDUE — see R007)
- A handwritten note reading "Emergency Bag #1"

No dangerous materials. Zero threat. Much cheese.

FINDINGS:
- Suitcase owner did not come back for it
- Appears to have been abandoned in a hurry
- Tag inside reads "Property of C. Bumblebee"
- Train to the coast departed Platform 7 at 2:17pm

CURRENT STATUS:
Suitcase held at Lost and Found.
Cheese donated to the station cafeteria (health regulations).
Penguin book returned to city library. Gerald very pleased.

Filed: February 14, 2024
Status: CLOSED — items in Lost and Found
