Interview conducted by: Detective A. Rivera
Date: March 15, 2024, 9:15 AM
Location: Agency conference room B

Witness: J. Park (neighbor, 4th floor, building adjacent to Agency)
Contact: On file with Agency

STATEMENT:
"I heard a loud bang around midnight. At first I thought it was the
pipes — this old building makes all kinds of noises. But then I heard
footsteps on the fire escape. Fast ones. Going UP, not down.
I looked out the window but only saw a shadow on the roof.
Then... nothing. They were just gone."

FOLLOW-UP QUESTIONS:
Q: Could you tell anything about the person's size or clothing?
A: "It was dark. But they moved fast. Athletic, maybe."

Q: Did you hear any voices?
A: "No. Just the footsteps and then silence."

INTERVIEWER NOTES:
Witness was cooperative and credible. No reason to doubt account.
Rooftop exit confirmed by this testimony and Photo 009. Consistent.

Witness contact information retained for possible follow-up.
