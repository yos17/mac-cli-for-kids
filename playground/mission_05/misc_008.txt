MISCELLANEOUS NOTES — FILE 008
Agency FAQ — For New Recruits

Q: What does the Terminal Detective Agency actually do?
A: We investigate mysteries, find missing things, and figure
   out what's going on when something suspicious is happening.
   Mostly we use computers, logic, and very good coffee.

Q: Do I need to know how to use a computer?
A: Yes. Especially the command line. It's more powerful than
   it looks. Ask any agent — they'll tell you.

Q: Is it dangerous?
A: Occasionally. Mostly it involves a lot of reading reports,
   drinking cold coffee at stakeouts, and trying to figure out
   who moved your cheese (sometimes literally).

Q: Who is Director Chen?
A: Director Chen runs the agency. She is very organized, drinks
   green tea instead of coffee (controversial), and once solved
   a case by noticing a protractor was 2 degrees off.

Q: Can I have a cape?
A: No. Absolutely not. We have a policy about this now.
   (See: General Order 7, "No Capes in Public or in the Office.")

Q: What should I do on my first day?
A: Read the case files. Learn the commands. Ask questions.
   Make good notes. Welcome to the agency.
